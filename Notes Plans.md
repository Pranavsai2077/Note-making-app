**Self-Emploted Professinals(SEP)**

Address Proof - Passport,Election ID, Utility bill, Driving license, Registered Rent Agreement, Aadhar Card

Age proof - Pan card, aadhar card

Income Proof - Latest 2 ITRs both peronal and business along with computation of income advance tax challans if available
